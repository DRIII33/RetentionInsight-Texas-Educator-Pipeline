# Dashboard Executive Summary: Educator Retention & Program Efficacy
---

#### **Data Analyst:** Daniel Rodriguez III 

#### **Date:** June 22, 2026

---

## Objective

The Texas PREP Allotment Dashboard serves as an automated diagnostic platform for monitoring program effectiveness, enabling the agency to transition from reactive headcount reporting toward proactive, risk-based educator retention strategies.

---

## Logic & Operations

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard aggregates metrics from the `looker_master_view` to provide a real-time assessment of statewide educator workforce stability and reporting confidence.

KPIs include:

- Total Teachers
- Retention Rate
- Mentorship Participation Rate
- Survey Response Rate
- Target Gap

---

### Chart 1: Longitudinal Retention Trends

Displays monthly retention rates against a 90% benchmark and segments results by educator preparation route.

**Purpose:**

- Monitor retention performance over time
- Evaluate program effectiveness
- Identify certification routes approaching or diverging from state goals

---

### Chart 2: Sentiment Risk Matrix

Implements a risk-based classification model that identifies **Detractors** when mentorship or administrative support scores are less than or equal to 2.

**Purpose:**

- Surface high-risk educators
- Reveal hidden dissatisfaction that average scores may conceal
- Support targeted intervention strategies

---

### Chart 3: Attrition Hotspots

Provides a ranked district-level priority index sorted by retention performance.

**Purpose:**

- Identify districts with the greatest retention challenges
- Prioritize support resources
- Inform localized policy and funding decisions

---

## Conclusion: Insights, Recommendations, & Next Steps

### Key Insights

The analysis indicates that high average sentiment scores can conceal underlying organizational friction points.

Districts exhibiting elevated Detractor counts consistently demonstrate lower retention rates, suggesting that school-level support structures are a significant driver of educator attrition.

---

### Recommendations

Shift PREP Allotment investments away from broad, statewide retention initiatives and toward targeted Detractor remediation strategies.

Priority should be given to districts exhibiting persistent challenges in:

- Administrative support
- Mentorship quality
- Educator engagement

---

### Next Steps

Integrate student performance outcomes into the analytical framework to determine whether districts demonstrating stronger educator retention also achieve measurable gains in student achievement.

This enhancement would establish a comprehensive program efficacy model connecting:

**Funding → Educator Support → Retention → Student Outcomes**
